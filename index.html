Our Story

Every lamp tells a story

Born from a love of traditional Indian craftsmanship and contemporary design, Spec Interior brings together artisans from across the country to create lighting and décor that transforms ordinary spaces into sanctuaries of warmth.

Each cane lamp is hand-cut, hand-painted, and assembled by skilled craftspeople who've spent generations perfecting their art. When you bring a Spec Interior piece home, you carry a piece of that heritage with you.
