Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our handcrafted cane products, shipping, and more.

🌿 Product & Craftsmanship
Are your products completely handmade?

Yes! Every single item at Spec Interior is crafted by hand by skilled artisans. Because they are handmade, no two pieces are 100% identical. Each item carries its own unique character and minor variations in weave, color, and texture.

Will the color of the cane change over time?

Cane and bamboo are natural, organic materials. Over time, exposure to natural light can cause the material to mature into a slightly deeper, richer golden tone. This is completely natural and adds to the vintage charm of the product.

How do I clean and maintain my cane products?

Cane is highly durable but loves a little care:

  • For daily dust: Use a soft, dry cloth or a soft-bristle brush to clean the dust out of the crevices.
  • For deeper cleaning: Wipe gently with a slightly damp microfiber cloth. Do not soak or use harsh chemical cleaners.
  • Keep it dry: Ensure the product stays away from prolonged moisture or direct rain to prevent mold.
💡 Lighting & Installation
Do your cane lamps come with electrical fixtures?

Yes, our cane pendant lights and lampshades come complete with standard electrical fixtures (holder, wire, and ceiling cap). Note: Bulbs are not included unless explicitly stated on the product page.

What kind of bulb should I use with a cane lampshade?

We highly recommend using warm LED bulbs. LEDs stay cool, making them completely safe for natural materials like cane and bamboo, while casting a beautiful, cozy shadow pattern on your walls.
